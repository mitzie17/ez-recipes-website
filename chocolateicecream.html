Very Chocolate Ice Cream

This chocolate ice cream is a rich, custard-style ice cream with no raw eggs!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 egg yolk, lightly beaten
  • 2 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

Step 1: Combine milk, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly.

Step 2: Place egg yolks into a small bowl. Gradually whisk in about 1/2 cup of hot milk mixture, then return to the saucepan. Heat and stir until thickened, but do not boil.

Step 3: Remove from the heat and stir in chopped chocolate until melted.

Step 4: Pour into a chilled bowl and refrigerate, stirring occasionally, until cold, about 2 hours.

Step 5: Stir in cream and vanilla. Pour into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
